About Us

A Legacy of Service and Leadership

Founded in 1998, the Rotary Club of Chennai Legends has been a beacon of hope and change in our community. We are a diverse group of professionals united by a common desire to give back.

Our club focuses on sustainable projects in education, health, and community development. Through fellowship and service, we strive to make a tangible difference in the lives of those we serve, guided by the principle of Service Above Self.

Our Focus

Where We Make a Difference

We channel our commitment to service at home and abroad through seven avenues of service, creating sustainable impact.

Peace & Conflict

Fostering understanding across borders and cultures to build a more peaceful world.

Disease Prevention

Educating and equipping communities to stop the spread of life-threatening diseases.

Water & Sanitation

Creating local solutions to bring clean water, sanitation, and hygiene to everyone.

Maternal & Child Health

Expanding access to high-quality care for mothers and children worldwide.

Basic Education

Promoting literacy to reduce gender disparity in education and empower communities.

Economic Development

Creating opportunities for productive work for young and old to alleviate poverty.

Protecting the Environment

Strengthening the conservation and protection of natural resources and ecological sustainability.
